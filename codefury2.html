CODEFURY


Team IEEE UVCE is proud to present IEEE UVCE Computer Society's inaugural Annual Hackathon "CodeFury 2.0" which is scheduled to take place on the 15th of September, 2019 at University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering, Bangalore following the 100th year of our Esteemed Institution. Our vision is to conduct Hackathons every year to help solve some of the pivotal problems everyone, as a technical society, face in day to day activities and to have a positive impact on the society at large.


"CodeFury 2.0" is a National level Hackathon which will draw participants from all over India. The main task of the Hackathon is to create a Web or Android Application related to the given problem domain. The problem domain would be given on the spot.
