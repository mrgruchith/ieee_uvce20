Team IEEE UVCE is proud to present IEEE UVCE Computer Society's inaugural annual Hackathon on the 15th of September, 2019 at University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering, Bangalore following the 100th year of our esteemed institution. The vision is to conduct hackathons every year to solve some of the pivotal problems everyone, as a technical society, face in day to day activities and to have a positive impact on the society at large.
